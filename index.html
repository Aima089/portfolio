Who I Am

Where technology
meets human dynamics

I'm a Computer Science graduate with a strong belief that the weakest link in any system isn't the technology — it's the connection between people and the tools they use. That conviction has shaped everything from my final-year AI project to my professional career.

At TwinSpider, I noticed decisions dragging and conflicts going unaddressed. Rather than treating it as a human failing, I traced the issue back to structural gaps in HR workflows and communication systems — then fixed them. At eFlow, I managed a fully remote team of 30 participants from different backgrounds, rebuilding digital workflows so that every task was transparent and accessible.

The result? Retention jumped from 60% to 85%, and 12 participants secured their first remote clients. I'm driven by cross-cultural collaboration, digital transformation, and building systems where people can genuinely thrive — wherever they are.
